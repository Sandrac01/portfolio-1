If you are on the hunt for a go-to hairstyle this summer, look no further. Goddess braids are a summertime favorite protective style for a reason. Goddess braids are essentially thicker cornrows. They are a great choice for natural hair because the low-maintenance style protects the hair from getting dry or tangled. Goddess braid hairstyles are bigger, raised higher, and braided closer to the scalp.They are unique in that they can be styled for every occasion; you can go from the gym straight to work, then out to drinks, all while protecting your hair and looking fresh.
